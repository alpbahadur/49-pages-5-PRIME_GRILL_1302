Our Story

A passion for excellence, tradition, and the art of fine dining

Where Tradition Meets Excellence

At PRIME GRILL 1302, we believe that great food starts with great ingredients. Nestled in the vibrant and upscale neighborhood of Condado in San Juan, Puerto Rico, our family-style steakhouse has become a cornerstone of culinary excellence in the area.

Our commitment is simple: provide our guests with the finest prime hand-cut meats and locally sourced ingredients from the best farms across Puerto Rico. Every dish tells a story of dedication, quality, and the rich culinary heritage of our island.

We pride ourselves on creating a warm, welcoming atmosphere where families, friends, and food lovers can gather to enjoy exceptional cuisine without pretension. This is fine dining with a family heart.
